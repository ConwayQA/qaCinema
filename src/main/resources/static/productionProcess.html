Production Process

This page describes an outline of our production process whilst developing this
website, please see the slides below for more detail.

Step One

We started by designing user stories from our clients specification, then using planning poker to decide our story points for each task

Step Two

Next we organised out user stories into a MoSCoW table so we could design our MVP

Step Three

We moved on by designing our first sprint from our backlog of user stories and assigned tasks to relevant persons

Step Four

Throughout Sprint One we maintained daily stand-up meetings via Microsoft Teams, discussing out progress and issues over the previous days

Step Five

At the end of Sprint One we discussed our Sprint Retrospective, here we discussed what we achieved and how will we improve moving forward onto our next sprint

Step Six

Next we checked our back-log of tasks and designed our next sprint to reach the MVP from the client specification

Step Seven

Through our second sprint we maintained our stand-up meetings in the same format from the first

Step Eight

We performed a Sprint Retrospective of our final sprint and we evaluated what we achieved and how we would improve in the future

Step Nine

MVP - we finished our initial prototype to the clients specification
